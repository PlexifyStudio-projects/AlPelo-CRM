1. FRESHA (fresha.com) — "The #1 Free Salon Software"
================================================================================

WHY THEY'RE #1:
Fresha dominates because they eliminated the subscription fee entirely.
Their model: free software + take a cut of transactions. This removes
the biggest barrier for small businesses: monthly SaaS fees.

TOP 5 KILLER FEATURES:
1. FREE CORE SOFTWARE — No monthly fee. Period. They monetize via
   payment processing (2.19%+$0.20 per transaction), new client
   marketplace commissions (20%), and optional paid add-ons.
2. MARKETPLACE / DISCOVERY — Fresha.com IS a consumer marketplace.
   Clients discover and book businesses directly. This is like being
   on Yelp + Booking.com + your own website simultaneously.
3. INTEGRATED PAYMENTS (Fresha Pay) — Tap-to-pay, card readers,
   online payments, no-show protection fees, deposits. The payment
   experience is seamless. No third-party payment processor needed.
4. POINT OF SALE — Full retail POS for selling products at the counter.
   Inventory tracking, barcode scanning, receipt printing.
5. AUTOMATED MARKETING — Email/SMS campaigns, Google Review requests,
   automated client recalls, birthday messages, all built in.

PRICING MODEL:
- Core software: FREE (unlimited staff, unlimited bookings)
- Fresha Pay: 2.19% + $0.20 per card transaction
- New client from marketplace: 20% of first visit
- Blast Campaigns (SMS/email): from $0.02/message
- Google Reviews boost: from $9.95/month
- Loyalty program: from $19.95/month
- Insights+ (advanced reporting): from $9.95/month

WHAT USERS LOVE (G2: 4.8/5, Capterra: 4.8/5):
+ Free to start — zero financial risk
+ Beautiful, intuitive UI — very low learning curve
+ The marketplace brings NEW clients organically
+ All-in-one: booking + POS + payments + marketing
+ Client app is excellent (easy for customers to use)
+ Fast onboarding (can be live in under 1 hour)

WHAT USERS HATE (weaknesses to exploit):
- 20% commission on new marketplace clients is STEEP
- Card processing fees add up fast for high-volume businesses
- Customer support is slow (email only, no phone for free tier)
- Limited customization — you get what they give you
- Reports are basic unless you pay for Insights+
- No WhatsApp integration (huge gap for LATAM)
- No AI assistant (no conversational intelligence)
- Multi-location management is clunky
- Cancellation/migration is difficult (data lock-in)

PLEXIFY ADVANTAGE OVER FRESHA:
>> Fresha has ZERO WhatsApp integration. In LATAM, that's fatal.
>> Fresha has NO AI assistant. Lina is a massive differentiator.
>> Fresha's marketplace commission model is predatory (20%). We can
   offer transparent flat pricing.
>> Fresha's support is notoriously slow. We can offer WhatsApp support.

4. BOULEVARD (joinblvd.com) — "Premium for Premium Businesses"
================================================================================

WHY THEY'RE "PREMIUM":
Boulevard targets high-end salons, med spas, and multi-location
businesses that charge premium prices. Their software reflects this:
enterprise features, premium UX, and higher pricing.

TOP 5 KILLER FEATURES:
1. PRECISION SCHEDULING — The most advanced scheduling engine in the
   industry. Auto-optimizes appointments to minimize gaps. Handles
   complex multi-service bookings with multiple providers. Resource
   and room assignment. Double-booking prevention with smart alerts.
2. OFFSET (PATENTED) — Their patented "Offset" feature automatically
   staggers appointments to fill schedule gaps. Example: if a color
   appointment has 30 min processing time, it auto-books another
   service during that gap. This maximizes revenue per hour.
3. CLIENT RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT — Deep client profiles with
   allergy alerts, medical history, before/after photos, formulas
   (color formulas for salons), detailed notes, lifetime value
   tracking, custom fields.
4. SELF-CHECKOUT — Clients can pay on their own device (shown a
   checkout link). Tips, split payments, packages, gift cards all
   handled. Reduces checkout bottleneck.
5. ENTERPRISE REPORTING — Revenue by location, staff utilization
   heatmaps, client retention cohorts, forecast modeling, custom
   report builder. This is genuinely enterprise-grade analytics.

PRICING MODEL:
- Essentials: ~$175/month (small businesses, limited features)
- Premier: ~$325/month (full features, up to 10 staff)
- Prestige: Custom pricing (multi-location, enterprise)
- Payment processing: 2.6% + $0.10

WHAT USERS LOVE (G2: 4.3/5, Capterra: 4.6/5):
+ Scheduling is the best in the industry (Offset is brilliant)
+ Beautiful, modern interface
+ Enterprise features without enterprise complexity
+ Self-checkout saves time at busy salons
+ Excellent API for custom integrations
+ Great for multi-location businesses

WHAT USERS HATE:
- EXPENSIVE — $175-325+/month is a lot for small businesses
- Onboarding takes 2-4 weeks (complex setup)
- Data migration from other platforms is painful
- No marketplace (doesn't bring new clients)
- Occasional bugs with the newer features
- No SMS/WhatsApp marketing (email only)
- Limited to US/Canada (no LATAM, no localization)
- Phone support only for Prestige tier

PLEXIFY ADVANTAGE OVER BOULEVARD:
>> Boulevard costs $175-325/mo. We can offer similar value at
   1/3 the price for LATAM market.
>> No WhatsApp. No AI assistant. No LATAM localization.
>> STEAL: Precision scheduling concept (gap optimization).
>> STEAL: Self-checkout via WhatsApp link (even better than their
   device-based approach).
>> STEAL: Client formula/recipe cards (useful for any service biz).

5. MANGOMINT (mangomint.com) — "The Fastest-Growing Challenger"
================================================================================

WHY THEY'RE GROWING FAST:
Mangomint is the "developer-friendly" salon software. They focus on
SPEED, INTEGRATIONS, and MODERN UX. They're the anti-Vagaro: fewer
features, but each one is polished to perfection. Their API-first
approach makes them the Stripe of salon software.

TOP 5 KILLER FEATURES:
1. SPEED — Fastest UI in the industry. Everything is instant.
   Calendar loads in milliseconds. Switching views is seamless.
   This sounds trivial but MATTERS enormously for daily use.
2. OPEN API / INTEGRATIONS — Full REST API. Zapier integration.
   Connect to ANYTHING: accounting, marketing, custom workflows.
   This is rare — most competitors have closed ecosystems.
3. SMART WAITLIST — When a cancellation happens, the system auto-
   notifies waitlisted clients in order and auto-books the first
   one who confirms. No manual work.
4. EXPRESS BOOKING — One-tap booking from the calendar. Drag to
   reschedule. Copy appointments. The booking flow has fewer clicks
   than any competitor.
5. INTELLIGENT FORMS — Digital intake forms with conditional logic.
   Answers auto-populate client profiles. Forms can trigger different
   workflows based on responses.

PRICING MODEL:
- Essentials: $165/month (unlimited staff, core features)
- Standard: $245/month (+ forms, waitlist, advanced features)
- Unlimited: $375/month (all features, priority support, API access)
- No per-staff fees. No processing fee markup (use your own processor).

WHAT USERS LOVE (G2: 4.9/5 — HIGHEST RATED, Capterra: 4.9/5):
+ Lightning fast — the calendar is a joy to use
+ Clean, modern design that doesn't feel cluttered
+ Flat pricing regardless of team size (great for large teams)
+ They actually LISTEN to customer feedback and ship fast
+ No contracts, cancel anytime
+ Bring-your-own payment processor (no markup on transactions)
+ Incredible customer support (responsive, knowledgeable)

WHAT USERS HATE:
- Expensive for solo practitioners ($165/mo minimum)
- No marketplace (doesn't bring new clients)
- No email/SMS marketing built in (relies on integrations)
- Limited reporting compared to Zenoti/Boulevard
- Relatively new company — some worry about longevity
- No mobile app (web-only, responsive)
- No WhatsApp anything
- US-only

PLEXIFY ADVANTAGE OVER MANGOMINT:
>> $165-375/mo is out of reach for LATAM businesses.
>> No WhatsApp, no AI, no LATAM presence.
>> STEAL: Their obsession with SPEED. Our calendar and UI must be
   lightning fast. This is a competitive moat.
>> STEAL: API-first approach. Open integrations win long-term.
>> STEAL: Smart waitlist concept (auto-fill cancellations).

WHAT USERS LOVE (G2: 4.4/5, Capterra: 4.3/5):

+ Mobile app is good (can run business from phone)
+ Portfolio feature is perfect for barbers
+ Affordable pricing for LATAM market

- No AI features at all
- CRM is very basic — just name, phone, notes

PLEXIFY ADVANTAGE OVER BOOKSY:
